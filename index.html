You built your onboarding process at 15 customers. You've got 100 now.

Most B2B SaaS companies treat onboarding as a project: a finite set of tasks to complete. That works when you have 10 customers and a hands-on CS team. It falls apart at 50, 100, 200 customers. The onboarding "project" becomes a bottleneck: customers stall, CS reps firefight, time-to-value stretches, and churn creeps in during the first 90 days.

The worst part? You can't see it happening until it's too late.

🚫

No visibility

You find out a customer is stuck when they complain or fail to renew. Your CS team tracks onboarding in spreadsheets and finds out about problems in weekly reviews, not in real time. By the time it surfaces, the customer has already mentally checked out.

📋

Every customer gets the same checklist

A 20-person startup and a 500-person enterprise go through the same onboarding. Your smaller customers feel overwhelmed. Your bigger customers feel underserved. Neither gets to value as fast as they should.

⏰

CS reps are drowning in admin

Your team spends more time updating trackers, sending reminder emails, and chasing stakeholders than actually helping customers succeed. And when something goes wrong, they're guessing at the fix rather than following a proven response.

Onboarding isn't a project. It's a product.

Onboarding should adapt to each customer the way the product itself does, using data about how that specific customer is engaging, not a generic playbook applied to everyone.

Dynamic Onboarding Plans

Every customer gets their own journey.

Clarion generates AI-powered onboarding plans that adapt based on the customer's segment, product usage, and engagement patterns. This isn't a template you pick from a dropdown. The plan changes as the customer progresses or stalls, pulling from patterns across your entire customer base.

A mid-market customer on a standard plan gets a streamlined path. An enterprise customer with complex integrations gets a high-touch journey with different milestones. The journey itself is intelligent.

AI-driven personalisation learned from patterns across your customer base. Not rule-based "if X then Y" logic.

Kova improved onboarding completion from 34% to 91%.

Adoption Tracking & Risk Signals

See every customer in real time. Catch problems before they surface.

One dashboard shows every customer, where they are in onboarding, and whether they're on track. Clarion pulls data from your product and integrations to build a real-time picture without relying on the CS rep to manually update a spreadsheet.

When a customer stalls (missed milestones, low engagement, delayed go-live), Clarion flags it automatically. Your team finds out when there's still time to intervene, not when the customer's already gone quiet.

Automated risk signals that catch stalling customers an average of 11 days earlier than manual tracking.

CS Team Playbooks

Know what to do when a customer stalls.

Visibility is only useful if your team knows what to do with it. When a risk signal fires, Clarion doesn't just flag the problem. It surfaces the recommended next action for the CS rep, based on what's worked for similar accounts in the past.

Your team stops guessing at the right response and starts following proven playbooks that improve with every customer you onboard. The result: faster interventions, more consistent outcomes, and CS reps who spend their time on high-impact work instead of admin.
